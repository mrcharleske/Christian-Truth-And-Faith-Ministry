About Our Ministry

Since our founding in late 2020, Christian Faith & Truth Ministry has grown into an impactful community dedicated to spreading God's love and truth.

Our Mission

To proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurture believers in their faith journey, and serve our community with love and compassion. We are committed to teaching biblical truth and helping people discover their purpose in God.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and truth in our community, raising disciples who will impact the world for Christ and establish His kingdom on earth.
